The Dunning-Kruger Effect

A cognitive bias in which unskilled individuals suffer from illusory superiority, mistakenly rating their ability much higher than average.

  • "My MySQL skills are about a 9 out of 10."
  • Even honest people will overrate themselves because they don't know what they don't know.
  • Do you have the expertise to know when that's happening? Do you have someone you trust to advise you?

Technical Debt

Taking out loans against your future performance, scalability, and maintainability in exchange for short-term gains in functionality.

  • Are we doing it "the right way"?
  • Is "the right way" worth it in this case?
  • What will this decision do to us in 6 months?
  • How much will it cost to pay down the debt?
  • PAY DOWN YOUR DEBT.
